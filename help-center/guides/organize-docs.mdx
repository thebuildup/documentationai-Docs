## Overview

Organizing your docs keeps your projects easy to navigate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Project">
    Click 'New Project' on your dashboard to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Use the 'Add Page' button to create sections and content for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Navigation Order">
    Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to organize structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
