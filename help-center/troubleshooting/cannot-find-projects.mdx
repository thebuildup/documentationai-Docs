---
title: Cannot Find My Projects
description: Fix missing projects in your documentation dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- No projects show on dashboard
- Project list appears empty

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Account">
    Make sure you are logged in with the correct account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the Page">
    Click the refresh button or press `Ctrl+R`/`Cmd+R`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    Try clearing the cache and refresh again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
