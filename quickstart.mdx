## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- No prior account on Roman Rudakov platform
</Callout>

## Sign Up for Your Account

Follow these steps to register and access the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://romanrudakov.com/signup` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a strong password. Use a valid email for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://romanrudakov.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up your first space to organize projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    From the main menu, select **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (`Public` or `Private`), and add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Theme" icon="palette">
    Choose your brand color `#3B82F6` and upload a logo if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Publish" icon="save">
    Click **Create Space** to generate your documentation hub.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Populate your space with content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Space" icon="book-open">
    Click on your new space from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Document" icon="plus">
    Select **New Document** and choose `quickstart.mdx` as the file name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add MDX Content" icon="code">
    Paste initial content using valid MDX syntax. Here's a sample:
    
````mdx
```javascript
console.log("Welcome to Roman Rudakov!");
```
````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Use the preview pane to check rendering, then hit **Publish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Interface Tour

Explore key areas using these tabs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Sidebar Navigation" icon="sidebar">
    The left sidebar lists your spaces and documents. Use search for quick access.
    
    - **Spaces**: Switch between projects
    - **Documents**: Edit or view files
    - **Settings**: Manage themes and users
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Editor Toolbar" icon="edit-3">
    Top toolbar provides:
    
    | Action     | Icon     | Description                  |
    |------------|----------|------------------------------|
    | Preview    | eye     | Toggle live preview          |
    | Publish    | upload  | Deploy changes               |
    | History    | clock   | View versions                |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Preview Pane" icon="monitor">
    Right pane shows rendered MDX with components like `<Callout>` and `<Steps>`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
